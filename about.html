About Authentic Ilm Archives

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

All Praise is due to Allah for allowing me to build and make this website accessible for my brothers and sisters.
This website is a resource for those seeking knowledge, at any level, to have easy access to PDFs and Lessons with just a few clicks. All the PDFs and resources provided on this website are upon the Salafi Manhaj, adhering strictly to the methodology of the pious predecessors. I do not support or promote the ideologies or works of Ahlul Bid’ah (the people of innovation).
The PDFs I provide are free of charge and non-copyrighted, ensuring they can be shared and accessed without any issues. The lessons linked with each PDF are carefully chosen to encourage studying under the guidance of Scholars and Ahlul Ilm, in line with the teachings of the Qur'an and Sunnah as understood by the Salaf.
The PDFs with lessons are organized to provide easy access to resources based on the category you wish to study, ensuring that beneficial knowledge is learned in a structured manner. PDFs without lessons are offered for casual reading, free of charge, for those who wish to benefit from them without requiring accompanying lessons.
May Allah allow you to benefit from this website, grant you sincerity in seeking knowledge, and increase you in beneficial knowledge. امين
