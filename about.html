About Authentic Ilm Archives

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

All Praise is due to Allah for allowing me to build and make this website accessible for my brothers and sisters.
This website is a resource for those seeking knowledge, at any level, to have easy access to PDF's and Lessons just with a few clicks.
The PDF's I provide are free of charge and Non-Copyrighted to ensure that they can be shared and accessed without any issues.
The Lessons which I link are paired with each PDF to ensure books are studied under the Scholars and Ahlul Ilm.
The PDF's with Lessons are there to provide easy access to the learning resources depending on the category you wish to study.
PDF's without Lessons are there for an easy read without the need of studying under lessons, without having to pay for the books.
May Allah allow you to benefit from this website and increase you in beneficial knowledge. امين
